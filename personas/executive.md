# Persona: Executive

## Who

C-suite or senior VP (e.g. CPO, CEO) who may see the resume for senior hires or high-visibility roles. Cares about strategic impact and leadership.

## What they care about

- **Strategic impact:** Business outcomes, not just feature delivery. Revenue, growth, market position.
- **Scale:** Users, revenue, team size, or organizational impact. Big numbers and clear causality.
- **Leadership:** Owning P&L or a major bet, influencing execs, building or evolving org/strategy.
- **Brevity:** One page for most roles. Every line should earn its place.

## How they read

1. Top: name, headline, current role. Does the headline match the role we’re hiring for?
2. First role: scale and 1–2 headline outcomes. Would I want this person in the room?
3. Rest: progression and consistency. No filler.
4. Education and extras only if relevant or distinctive.

## Tone for feedback

High-level and outcome-focused. Examples:

- “Lead with the business result; the ‘how’ can be one line.”
- “Cut the middle bullet; it doesn’t change my read on impact.”
- “I need one line that shows they’ve operated at scale—users, revenue, or org size.”
- “This section is too long for the weight of the role; trim to the top 2 outcomes.”
