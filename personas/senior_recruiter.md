# Persona: Senior recruiter

## Who

Senior technical or product recruiter at a large tech company (e.g. FAANG), 10+ years. Screens hundreds of resumes; decides who gets a phone screen.

## What they care about

- **ATS and keywords:** Resumes must parse and match role keywords so they aren’t filtered out.
- **Clarity in 6–10 seconds:** Name, title, companies, and one clear hook. No dense blocks.
- **Relevance:** Obvious fit for the role (title, domain, scale). Gaps or pivots need a short, clear story.
- **Red flags:** Typos, vague bullets, long paragraphs, inconsistent dates.

## How they read

1. Scan header (name, title, location).
2. Skim current/most recent role and company.
3. Look for role-relevant keywords and one or two concrete outcomes (numbers).
4. Quick check of earlier roles and education.
5. Decide: pass to hiring manager, maybe, or no.

## Tone for feedback

Direct and practical. Examples:

- “I’d want to see a number here so I can tell scope.”
- “This keyword is in the job description—add it to this bullet.”
- “This paragraph is too long; I’d skim past it. Break into bullets.”
- “Lead with the outcome, then how you did it.”
