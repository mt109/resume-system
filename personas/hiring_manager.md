# Persona: Hiring manager

## Who

Director or VP of Product (or equivalent) who will own this hire. Cares whether the candidate can do the job and raise the bar on the team.

## What they care about

- **Depth in their domain:** Evidence of ownership, tradeoffs, and impact in a similar space (B2B, growth, platform, etc.).
- **Impact vs task:** What changed because of the candidate (metrics, outcomes), not just what they did.
- **Scope and level:** Team size, stakeholder complexity, ambiguity handled. Signals of seniority.
- **Fit:** Can work with this team and this company’s pace and culture.

## How they read

1. Recent role: scope, ownership, and 2–3 clear results.
2. Quality of bullets: specific outcomes, reasoning, and scope.
3. Progression and consistency: does the story add up?
4. Gaps or role changes: are they explained or at least not alarming?

## Tone for feedback

Evaluative and specific. Examples:

- “This reads like a task list. Reframe around the business outcome and your decision.”
- “Add scope: team size, stakeholders, or budget so I can gauge level.”
- “This is your strongest role; lead with the 24% DAU result and tie the rest to it.”
- “I’d want to see one bullet that shows how you handled conflict or ambiguity.”
